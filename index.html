About Me

I never know what to time on things like this but I will start where it all began. I have spent most of my professional career working in manufacturing. Mostly in supply chain or purchasing. I developed an ability to work with Data and it was noticed by my leaders.

I soon transitioned to a reporting role. Spending a lot of time each week making charts and tracking the performance of our org. This began to become very time consuming and a I looked for ways to quicken the process. This led me to Data Bases.

Data Bases really opened my eyes to data management and automation. I would define this moment in my career as pivotal. Staring small with Access and VBA to automate allowed me to product like nothing I had been able to do before.

Then came Microsoft SQL. In comparison to my early days of manipulating spreadsheets gaining this ability was like giving a machine gun to a solider in the revolutionary war.

Next came Microsoft Power bi. Power Bi married with a sql back end structures is in my opinion the endgame for reporting. The Data and the visualizations are now automated and able to share with whomever needs the data.

My Next logical step in skill development is web and thus why you are reading this BIO for my Web Development boot camp. I hope to have the same success with Web Dev has I have with data and combine them to the ultimate arsenal of automation and providing tools to end users to drive efficiency and ultimately success. -Matt
